Activities.

Physical Activities

Physical education holds a pivotal role in our holistic approach to learning. Recognizing its vast benefits, we've integrated a comprehensive physical education program tailored for our students.The school grounds serve as a playground for various sports, ensuring active participation. Beyond mere games, our curriculum focuses on fostering a healthy lifestyle, building resilience, and promoting sportsmanship. At Royal Public School, physical education isn't just an activity; it's a foundation for a balanced and enriched student life.

  • Intra and Inter-school Competitions : Our annual sports day, regular sports meets, and participation in inter-school tournaments ensure that our students get ample competitive exposure, fostering a spirit of sportsmanship and camaraderie.

Dance

Children are talented differentially. The school identifies the various talents in each child & encourages them by providing a platform to exhibit their talents.We recognize dance as a powerful form of self-expression, an art that unites the body and mind, and a discipline that instills dedication and perseverance in students. Our dedication to nurturing and promoting dance is evident in our comprehensive dance program.
Every year, students get the opportunity to showcase their talent and hard work through annual recitals, allowing them to experience the thrill of live performances.We encourage our students to participate in inter-school dance competitions, giving them a platform to compete, learn, and grow.

Yoga

Yoga is the journey of the self, through the self, to the self. Children derive enormous benefit from yoga physically; it enhances their flexibility strength, co-ordination & body awareness. In addition their concentration and sense of calmness and relaxation improves. It reduces strength and anxiety and improves memory and a attention span. “Yoga brings that marvelous inner light that all children have to the surface.”

Painting

At Royal Public School, we deeply value the arts and the profound impact they have on individual expression and creativity. Our school is elated to provide an extensive painting program for our enthusiastic young artists. Through painting, we introduce students to a world where colors and strokes come together to narrate powerful stories and evoke emotions. With dedicated art studios and seasoned instructors, we ensure that every student's artistic potential is nurtured and celebrated. Our curriculum encompasses various painting techniques, from traditional watercolors to modern acrylics, allowing students to explore and find their unique style.

Karate

Karate, the martial art benefits students at every aspect of their life; Physically mentally spiritually, emotionally, developing balance, co-ordination, focus, respect, discipline, self-defense, etc., The school provides Karate training to all the students for grade I to X under the guidance of the Karate National Coach, and Founder and Technical Director of Indian Shotokon Karate-Do Association.
Karate with deep-rooted traditions, provides a perfect blend of physical training and mental fortitude. Our qualified karate instructors, with their vast expertise, mentor students through various katas, forms, and sparring techniques.
